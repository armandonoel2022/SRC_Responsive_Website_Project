Our Services

Perimeter Security

Security officers with or without weapons, to protect lives, assets and property delimited by a defined perimeter, in order to prevent attacks or entry by intruders or unauthorized persons.

Female Staff

Our Security team also integrates female personnel who very professionally and responsibly carry out their access control tasks in an impeccable manner, always ensuring faithful compliance with the established rules and procedures.

VIP Protection

Qualified and trained personnel for special missions, such as Bodyguards, Security drivers, etc.

Intervention patrols

Armed response team in 4x4 vehicles or motorcycles, to provide assistance and peace of mind while the breakdown of your vehicle or any other situation is solved.

Escorts

We can guarantee the security during the displacement of Executive Personnel, their escorts of materials or equipment.

Armored vehicles

We have vehicles with high levels of armor, for when the need so requires.

Electronic Security

Wireless alarm systems

PowerMaster-10

(Ideal for homes and apartments) is a compact, wireless home security system. Using extremely secure wireless technology, it offers superior coverage, long battery life, and unmatched wireless robustness.

Wireless alarm systems

PowerMaster-30

(Specially designed for offices) is a professional wireless security alarm system. Suitable for advanced high-performance operations and control alarm security applications, it features superior range, long battery life and unmatched wireless robustness. In addition, it provides excellent wireless functionality with all peripherals.

Security Camera Systems

We put at your disposal our wide range of products and integrated surveillance and security systems with high efficiency in their performance, always adjusted to your needs and your budget.

Panic button

It is a small device that is light and easy to carry, which allows you to contact our Monitoring Center in emergency cases by simply pressing the SOS button, since it allows two-way communication; It also allows you to send messages (SMS) simultaneously to the numbers that have been previously programmed. The equipment supplies the exact position at the moment of being pressed.

With just two simple buttons and no keyboard, waterproof, it's one of the friendliest devices on the market.

GPS Tracking

We offer satellite monitoring services for vehicles, containers, packages, briefcases, among others, facilitating their location in real time, through our platform or Smartphone.

Patrol Drones

We use these electronic devices to carry out security missions, perimeter rounds, events, demonstrations and reconnaissance, which allow us to reach difficult places very quickly and have a more precise vision of the events at the moment; This information is very useful for rapid intervention teams on the ground.

Monitoring Center

A team of professionals from our company works 24/7 to monitor the performance of our clients' alert systems (alarms, cameras, panic buttons, etc.), and they are available to help them with any need that may arise.

Other services we offer

Information Systems Security

The most valuable thing that every organization has is its information, therefore keeping it well protected is of vital importance, for them we have specialists in Computer Security to guarantee their computerized resources (Online Backup, Password Manager, etc).

Maintenance of electronic equipment

This service includes the visit of a technician every month to verify the status of the equipment, perform cleaning and conditioning of devices, verification of backup copies, instruction to a user who needs some support, adjustments or configurations for proper functioning and a general report. current state of the systems.

Special Investigations

We can carry out inquiries of your interest in order to minimize the risks of people with a doubtful record entering your organization (police and judicial clearances, home visits, laboratory tests, etc.) and other highly confidential cases.

Security Consulting

Risk analysis with recommendations to improve your security systems, security plans, etc.

Trainings

We offer preventive security talks, with the aim of making your employees more reactive to everyday dangers and minimizing the risks of them being victims of assaults, robberies, express kidnappings, electronic fraud, etc.
