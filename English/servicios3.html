Panic button

It is a small device that is light and easy to carry, which allows you to contact our Monitoring Center in emergency cases by simply pressing the SOS button, since it allows two-way communication; It also allows you to send messages (SMS) simultaneously to the numbers that have been previously programmed. The equipment supplies the exact position at the moment of being pressed.

With just two simple buttons and no keyboard, waterproof, it's one of the friendliest devices on the market.

GPS Tracking
